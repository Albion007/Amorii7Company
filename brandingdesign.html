Branding Design


While not the same thing as branding, web design is informed by your brand design.

Branded Website

Here are the 6 rules of a branded website

1. Define who you are. What you look like.

You use yourself as the object to refer to the second person (you) when the subject already contains the second person (you) .Your voice, your style, your network, your habits, your word, consistency these things can define who you are.

2. Create a logo. Keep your logo simple.

Provide information about your business. Describe your product, target audience and industry, describe your brand values, provide your company name, design style, colors, inspiration etc. Keep it as simple as you can to make it look cute.

3. Choose great photos. Images convey emotion.

Emotion in a photos, or any work of art, is what helps a viewer connect with a piece if that emotion is prevalent in the viewer ... Emotion, or a feeling, is what can bring a snapshot out of obscurity and make it shine. Simple photos...

4. Keep your website simple.

Your goal for your company’s website should be to engage readers quickly and to get them to click that “contact us” button. The key to doing this quickly is to keep your website simple. Here are a few of our favorite tips for keeping your site engaging without being too overwhelming.

5. Make it mobile. Make it Responsive.

Make Your Website Responsive. Make Information People Look for Easier to Find. Ditch Text-Blocking Ads and Pop-ups. Make Website Speed a Priority. Keep Your Web Design Simple. Make Your Button Sizes Large Enough to Work on Mobile. Use Large Font Sizes. Don't Use Flash.

6. Use SEO strategies to help people find you.

SEO can help you build better relationships with your audience, improve the customer experience, increase your authority, drive more people to your site, give you an edge over the competition, and increase conversions, which means more sales, more loyal customers, and more growth for your business.
